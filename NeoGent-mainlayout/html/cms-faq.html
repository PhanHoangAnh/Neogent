How can we help you?

No shipping confirmation?

At the moment we are experiencing delays in sending out shipping confirmations. Please contact our Customer Service if you have any questions. We apologise for the inconvenience this may cause you.

Where is my order?

After you place an order we’ll send you an email. This will remind you what's in your order, where your parcel is being delivered and all the legal stuff like terms and conditions. You will also receive tracking information by email when your order has been dispatched.

To view past and outstanding orders and generally enjoy a quicker and simpler ordering process you can create your own user account. Sign up and log in by clicking ‘My Account’ in the menu on the left of the site.

NOTE: It can take 36-48 hours before you can track the parcel. To keep your shipping costs down we send all packages from our warehouse in Germany together in one truck. Therefore, the tracking can only start once the package has arrived and been sorted in your country.

I've changed my mind. Can I change my order?

To get things moving we like to send all orders straight to our warehouse for packing and posting as quickly as we can. So once your order is complete and through the checkout, you can’t make any changes.

If you have ordered something you don’t want to keep, just send it back using the prepaid return label included with each parcel.
