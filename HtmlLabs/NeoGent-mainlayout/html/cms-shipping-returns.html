Shipping

Deliveries will be made within 3-5 working days from order placement.

  • Day 0: Order confirmation email is sent within minutes.
  • Day 0-1: Your order is being prepared to be shipped.
  • Day 0-2: Your package is on its way and you receive an email with a tracking link.
  • Day 3-5: You receive your order (95% of our packages arrive by Day 3).
  • Day 7-14: Help us improve - Tell us what we can do better.

*Order placed on Friday after 12:00 will be shipped on Monday.

Returns

How do I return my item(s)?

  • Fill out the easy return form included in your package.
  • Schedule a pick-up with UPS and your return is on its way.*
  • After your return arrives we will issue a refund to your account within 2 working days.
  • You receive a refund receipt email.
  • Money is in your account within 2-7 days.**

*It may take up to 10 working days for us to receive your return.
**The time it takes for your refund to appear in your bank statement is influenced by your payment issuer and is out of our control.
**Over 90% of our customers will see their refund within 2-7 days.
